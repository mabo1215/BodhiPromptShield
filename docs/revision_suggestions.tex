\subsection*{Mandatory Revision 6: Strengthen Related Work B}
Please expand subsection III-B with one more paragraph making the distinction between:
\begin{itemize}
    \item memorization risk;
    \item prompt-time leakage;
    \item interface-level exposure.
\end{itemize}

Suggested insertion text:
\begin{verbatim}
It is important to distinguish prompt-time exposure from model memorization. Memorization concerns whether a model can reproduce sensitive content from its training data, whereas prompt-time exposure concerns whether sensitive user inputs are directly observable by the deployed system and its surrounding infrastructure. The latter risk arises even when the model was not trained on the user's private data and is therefore especially relevant for practical agent deployment.
\end{verbatim}
