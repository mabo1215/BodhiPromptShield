\section{External Datasets, Baselines, and Provenance Resources}
\label{app:external_resources}

To ensure reproducibility and external validity, this study references
licensed clinical corpora, publicly available OCR-heavy datasets,
and executable baseline pipelines. Table~\ref{tab:external_resources}
summarizes the major resources and their official access URLs. The
repository now also records these access paths through a machine-readable
acquisition manifest and an OCR-heavy transfer scaffold, but those
artifacts should still be read as provenance support rather than as
executed benchmark evidence.
