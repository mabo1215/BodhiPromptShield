\section*{What Is Significantly Better in This Version}

\subsection*{1. The paper now has a clearer scientific stance}

\begin{enumerate}

\end{enumerate}

这条主线在摘要、引言、方法、结果、结论中都已经相对一致。:contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

\subsection*{2. The formalization of $\Pi$ is now much more convincing}

\[
\hat L_{\text{privacy}}(m \mid z_i)=\alpha_1 \cdot \text{DirectExposureRisk}+\alpha_2 \cdot \text{ContextLeakageRisk}+\alpha_3 \cdot \text{RestorationSurface}

以及

\[
\hat L_{\text{utility}}(m \mid z_i)=\beta_1 \cdot \text{ExactValuePenalty}+\beta_2 \cdot \text{SemanticDrift}+\beta_3 \cdot \text{LatencyPenalty}

你已经把这个 selector 从“概念上的 policy function”推进成了“可由 rule-instantiated approximations 实现的系统控制器”。:contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}
